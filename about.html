About us

We want to provide you with food and drink that is healthier than your average cafe (we don't sell sausages or white bread) - in smaller portions (if that's what you want) in a cosy, homely, happy environment.

We'll provide you with information and advice about food and drink. We recycle as much of our waste as possible.
