Events

We hold weekly and monthly events as well as pop-up events

Our Weekly Events

Knit & Natter

The Knit and Natter Group is looking for new members. Come along on a Wednesday morning, bring your knitting or crochet. If you don't know how to crochet or knit there will be someone to guide you.

Drop-in Meditation class

Every Wednesday Evening from 7:15 PM - 9-45 PM the class is run by Solihull Buddhists (an offshoot of the Birmingham Buddhists Group in Moseley). No need to book, just turn up with an open mind wearing comfortable clothes. Donations for the class are invited(£8 suggested).

Guitar Live

Join the "Guitar Live" group on the second Tuesday of the month 8 - 10 PM. all players welcome no matter your skill level. Bring your guitar and perform for the group, join in a group song or just listen. Refreshments are available all evening.

On evenings when we have a guest performer (every 2-3 months) please book a seat in advance and do not bring your guitar.

Fiction Night

Fiction nights have become a very popular event at our cafe, With local authors coming along to read their Novel, poems and short stories. each evening is different with many of the authors writing a piece especially for the event.
Entry on the night is free and tea, coffee, soft drinks and cake available before the reading and at the interval.
The night starts at 7 Ppm and runs until 10 pm with up to 10 people reading

Our pop-up events

Vegan pop-up

Every month, on a Thursday or Friday we have a Vegan Pop-Up Restaurant at 7:30 pm, with food by our favourite plant-based chef Rachel Leigh-Janney (Herbalicious Foods) or Cafe Owner, Nicolette. Tickets are £15 and must be booked and paid for in advance (these events always sell out, so book your tickets early). If you want to pay via bank transfer - please call the cafe and we will text you the bank details. Next dates: Friday 9th August 2019 - Tickets Available Further dates to be announced
